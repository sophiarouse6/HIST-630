We learn that steps have already been taken by the looking toward the realization of the project facilitated by the opportune State aid and also tending toward inducing the Legislature to still farther promote the cause of immigration by placing the financially in a condition which would extend the sphere of its usefulness.

At a meeting of the Board of Directors of the German Society , a few days since, it was resolved that a committee com posed of the President, Vice President and secretary of the society be appointed, whose duty it shall be to select a suitable site for the proposed immigrant depot. Another resolution was adopted directing the President of the society to appoint a committee of three, whose duty it shall be to induce the Legislature to authorize the division of the present head tax upon immigrants arriving at our port equally between the and the Charity Hospital
